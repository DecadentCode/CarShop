About Us

At Aurora Autoworks, we are dedicated to breathing new life into classic and antique automobiles. Our team of skilled craftsmen and automotive enthusiasts combines decades of experience with a deep-rooted passion for preserving automotive history. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional restorations, ensuring that every car that leaves our Westland, Michigan facility is a testament to the golden era of motoring. With unparalleled attention to detail and a commitment to providing personalized customer service, Aurora Autoworks is your premier destination for all your classic car restoration needs. Rediscover the timeless elegance and thrill of the open road with us.
